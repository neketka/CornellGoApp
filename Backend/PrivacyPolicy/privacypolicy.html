Mode and Place of Processing the Data

CornellGO sends location data to our servers to enable the hot/cold bar, find new challenges, and update your visited places. We associate the predetermined location of each landmark with your account only when you approach it. Your specific location will be used by our servers to determine your distance to a landmark and will not be retained for any purpose afterwards. Your location is not collected. By registering, you consent to this and any other form of data collection explicitly requested by the application (including, but not limited to, usernames and email addresses).
